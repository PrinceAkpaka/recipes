Cynthia's Yam and Plantain

Ingredients

  • Yam
  • Plantain
  • Egg

Honestly, I have little to no knowledge on how this meal is created. I just watch my mother fry the plantain, fry the yam, and warm up the premade egg. Here is a list of what I see her do when I happen to be in the kitchen with her.

  1. Put oil in a frying pan
  2. Place the frying pan over a burner and start the burner
  3. Cut open the mound of yam and the plantain
  4. Place the slices into the frying pan
  5. Wait a couple of minutes..
  6. When you start to see a brownish color on the plantains, remove the yam and plantain from the pan
  7. Warm up your egg
  8. Voila. Enjoy
